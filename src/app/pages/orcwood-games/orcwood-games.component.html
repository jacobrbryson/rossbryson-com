Orcwood Games: My Journey as Owner & CEO

As the Owner and CEO of Orcwood Games, I've had the privilege of leading a passionate team in the development and creation of engaging and innovative gaming experiences. My involvement spans strategic direction, project oversight, and contributing to the technical and creative vision that brings our games to life.

Highlights of My Involvement

  • Strategic Leadership: Guiding the overall vision and business strategy for Orcwood Games, ensuring sustainable growth and creative excellence.
  • Product Development Oversight: Overseeing the development lifecycle of multiple game titles, from concept to launch, including games like Oxeblood, Trivia Crawler, New America, and many more.
  • Team Building & Management: Fostering a collaborative environment and managing a talented team of developers, artists, and audio engineers.
  • Technical & Creative Direction: Providing input on game design, technical architecture, and ensuring high-quality execution across all projects.
  • Business Operations: Managing the operational aspects of a game development studio, including partnerships, marketing, and financial planning.
