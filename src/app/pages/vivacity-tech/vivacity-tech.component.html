My Role at Vivacity Tech

As a key contributor at Vivacity Tech, I've been deeply involved in developing and enhancing solutions that streamline technology management for K-12 schools. My work focuses on creating robust, scalable, and user-friendly platforms that address the unique challenges of 1:1 device deployments, from procurement and deployment to repairs and comprehensive asset tracking.

Key Contributions & Focus Areas

  • V Suite Pro Platform: Contributing to the development of our all-in-one platform, designed to provide complete coverage for K-12 device lifecycle needs.
  • Device Lifecycle Management: Building features that support efficient device procurement, deployment strategies, and robust repair workflows.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Working on systems that provide schools with real-time data on device health and usage, enabling proactive management.
  • Integration with Google for Education: Ensuring seamless integration with Google Admin console and leveraging Google Cloud for secure, scalable solutions.
  • Optimizing School Operations: Developing tools that significantly reduce the time tech teams spend on device logistics, allowing them to focus on educational support.

Highlighting Dream™ Asset Management

A significant part of my work involves supporting and enhancing Dream™ by Vivacity, our proprietary asset management platform built exclusively for K-12 schools. Dream™ is designed to be the central hub for managing Chromebook fleets, offering:

  • Real-time insights into device health and usage.
  • Geo-tracking capabilities for device location.
  • Streamlined workflows for repair and inventory management.
  • Proactive tools to prevent device issues and ensure FERPA compliance.
